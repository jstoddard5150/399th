Military Ceremonies

The 399th Army Band supports official military ceremonies both on and off of Fort Leonard Wood. In order to book the the 399th Army Band for an official military performance, download This Form and email it to 399th Army Band Operations Office.

Public Performance Request

The 399th Army Band also supports many not for profit civilian functions throughout the state of Missouri, and travels throughout the midwest in support of community relations. To request the 399th Army Band for a non-military performance, contact the Fort Leonard Wood Public Affairs Office at (573) 563-4145, or visit their website for more information including fax and email options.

General Information

If you need general information about the 399th Army Band please feel free to contact us at:

  • Front Desk: (573) 596-0686
  • S-3 Operations: (573) 596-1360
  • Fax: (573) 596-0687

Need more information?

Utilization of the 399th Army Band is subject to the requirements listed in FM 220-90-3 as well as other applicable regulations. Please contact the Band Operations Supervisor if you have any questions.
